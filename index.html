Deep River Campground is a beautiful scenic hillside park, located on The Deep River. We also have a 5 Acre Stocked Fishing Lake, and 20 Acres of groomed nature trails. Near the Zoo but also convenient to Greensboro, we offer cabins and Full Service RV Sites, Our Pool on the hillside has the Best View in Town ! We also have laundry on site, a game room, play ground, and camp store. We are the only campground with a Large Riverfront Park with an Historic Dam built in the mid- 1800's and a small Riverfront Beach with Beach Chairs.
